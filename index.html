IIT-Mandi Indian Languages dataset


This dataset contains speech samples from 9 Indian Languages. The languages are: Assamese, Bengali, Gujarati, Hindi, Kannada, Malayalam, Manipuri, Odia and Telugu. This datset was created for developing a spoken language identification sytem in Indian languages. These samples are obtained from YouTube videos on education and personal interviews. The nature of speech is spontaneous speech.

There are 6 speakers (3 male and 3 female speakers) in every language. Each speech sample has a duration around 10 seconds. Approximately 30 mins of speech is available for every language. Since the recordings were obtained from wide variety of topics, some samples contain English words mixed with the original speech (code-mixed speech). Also, some samples contain background noise (moderate level of noise). All file have sampling rate of 8 kHz.
